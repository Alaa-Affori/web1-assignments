About Me

I am Ala'a Affori, a CAP student at An-Najah National University. My enthusiasm for technological advancements motivates me to join this program. Beyond my academic interests, I am deeply committed to voluntary work. For example, I volunteered as a Content Writer and Secretary at the IEEE An-Najah Student Branch, where I had the honor of being selected as the IEEE Day Ambassador in 2022. Additionally, I volunteered at the language resource center (LRC), teaching Arabic to international volunteers. I find joy and fulfillment in volunteering and making a positive impact in my community as I believe in the power of collaboration!
